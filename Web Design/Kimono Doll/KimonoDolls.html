Come Learn About Japaneese Dolls!

Japanese dolls will be featured this year at the clutural festival held the last weekend of May at the fairgrounds. Hundreds of participants will provide traditonal Japanese food, clothing, and dolls. The highlight is the display of Kimono dolls. Bring the whole family for a day of fun!

Activities:

  • Traditonal costumes on display and for sale
  • Kimono Doll show
